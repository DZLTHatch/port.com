My Journey So Far
As someone who has worked in multiple fields over the past four years, I've learned a lot about myself and the industry. Starting out as a voice over artist, I quickly realized that there was more to my career than just lending my voice to a project. With time, I discovered my passion for social media marketing and web design, which have now become integral parts of my professional life. In this blog post, I'll share my journey and some insights that might be helpful to those just starting out in these fields. Voice Over Artist My career as a voice over artist began when I was approached by a local production house to lend my voice to a commercial. I was thrilled at the prospect of working in the entertainment industry, and that one project led to many more. Over the years, I've voiced radio and TV commercials, explainer videos, e-learning courses, audiobooks, and even video games. It's a fascinating job that requires a lot of hard work, practice, and patience. While some people think it's just about talking into a microphone, it's much more than that. As a voice over artist, I've had to learn how to read scripts, take direction, and master the art of timing and delivery. It's a competitive industry, but it's also very rewarding when you get it right. Social Media Specialist As I gained more experience in the entertainment industry, I realized the importance of social media marketing. I saw how social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter could be used to promote my work as a voice over artist, and I wanted to learn more. I started taking courses on digital marketing and social media, and soon enough, I landed my first job as a social media specialist for a local start-up. In this role, I was responsible for creating content, managing social media accounts, and analyzing data to improve engagement. It was a challenging role that taught me a lot about the power of social media, and I soon realized that this was a field I wanted to pursue further. Web Designer As I became more involved in social media marketing, I also started to take an interest in web design. I saw how websites could be used to showcase my work as a voice over artist, and I wanted to learn more about the process of designing and developing a website. I started taking courses on web design and HTML, and soon enough, I was designing websites for clients. In this role, I was responsible for creating layouts, designing graphics, and ensuring that the website was user-friendly and responsive. It was a challenging role that required a lot of attention to detail, but it was also very fulfilling to see my designs come to life.
